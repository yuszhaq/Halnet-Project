About us

We are leading provider in telcom infrasctructure and services across the country and beyond

HALNET iS a premier provider of telecom infrastructure services, specializing in civil and telecom work, particularty in fiber optics development. Committed to quality and reliability, HALNET delivers tailored solutions to meet diverse client needs, making us a trusted partner in the telecommunications industry.
